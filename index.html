Innovation

Bangladesh slips further in Global Innovation Index | The ... Bangladesh has been making progress in innovation, but its performance is still lower than many other countries. The country's innovation efforts include inventions, initiatives, and awards.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh involves efforts to meet current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. This includes efforts to protect natural resources, ensure fair labor practices, and promote economic benefits.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure includes roads, bridges, railways, waterways, and energy projects. The country has made significant investments in infrastructure in recent years, but there is still a need for more. So, Bangladesh need more and more manpower.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh has focused on improving women's rights, education, and economic participation. The government has taken steps to increase women's representation in politics and the workforce, and to improve their health and safety.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation in Bangladesh is the process of using technology to change the country's business processes, culture, and customer experiences.

Green Energy

Green energy in Bangladesh is energy that comes from renewable sources like sunlight, wind, and water. It is produced without harming the environment.
