Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh involves efforts to meet current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. This includes efforts to protect natural resources, ensure fair labor practices, and promote economic benefits.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure includes roads, bridges, railways, waterways, and energy projects. The country has made significant investments in infrastructure in recent years, but there is still a need for more.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh has focused on improving women's rights, education, and economic participation. The government has taken steps to increase women's representation in politics and the workforce, and to improve their health and safety.
